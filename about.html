About Us

Campus PFA (People for Animals) at Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology, established in 2019 as a chapter of PFA India, is a student-led initiative committed to the care and welfare of animals on the TIET campus. Our activities include regular feeding, vaccinations, deworming, sterilization, and providing first aid to animals in need. We also facilitate adoptions to help animals find safe and loving homes. Through these efforts, Campus PFA works to foster a compassionate and responsible campus environment where animals are treated with kindness and respect.
